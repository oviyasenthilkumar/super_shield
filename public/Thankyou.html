Supershield Flexstrip

  • Home
  • Products
  • Jointing Systems and Auxiliary
  • Flexstrip

TPE-based elastic dilation tape and elastic tapes for construction joints

The Supershield Flexstrip range of sealing tapes exhibits exceptional flexibility and reliable, durable sealing solutions. They are specifically designed to seal both horizontal and vertical junctions as well as cracks that may occur due to repeated contractions and expansions, which are particularly effective in harsh environmental conditions that can cause damage.

Product Range

Supershield Flexstrip 250

TPE Based, Elastic Dilatation Tape

FLEXSTRIP 250 Dilatation Tape is a 250 mm wide, highly elastic elastomer designed for the insulation of expansion joint gaps, large cracks, and thermal gaps in all types of buildings. It is widely used in foundations, walls, wastewater treatment plants, water tanks, tunnels, and concrete pipes. The tape is resistant to UV light and external weather conditions, providing reliable performance across a wide range of temperatures. FLEXSTRIP 250 is available in thicknesses of 1 mm, 1.5 mm, and 2 mm, with options for versions with holes and without holes.

Supershield Flexstrip LF 120

Elastic Tapes for Construction Joints

FLEXSTRIP LF 120 Tape, made from a thermoplastic elastomer polyester mesh, is designed for isolating critical points in construction. It is ideal for use in wet areas such as bathrooms and toilets, as well as watertight constructions, roofs, and parapets.
